Membership Guidelines

All members must be 18 or older to play (at least 21 to drink alcohol).

Have appropriate attire.

Must provide guarantee of staying close to our local community by living in the neighbourhood upon entry at the time of sign up.

All members are expected to behave in a polite orderly manor.

No drunken or disrespectful behaviour will be tolerated.
