Who we are

THE NUT LOW is a site dedicated for people who are interested in playing Texas Holdem poker in Stockholm, Sweden.

This is a member area only, created to make it easier for members of The KPC United Poker Club to meet, socialise and enjoy the game!

KPC United was founded in 2010 but was originally conceived in 2008. KPC is the brainchild of Thomas Nord and Ola Sand√©n and is a locally operated poker society in the southwest suburbs of Stockholm.

Combining several years of extensive poker experience, we love to meet and hang out together as well as trying to improve on our game, KPC aims to provide the best poker experience for our members!

For beginners, sharks, and everyone in between, we're dedicated to having fun, making friends, and sharing our love for poker!
