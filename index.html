Gameplay


Texas HoldEm

To begin with, the two players left of the button post forced bets called "blinds". After two cards have been dealt to each player, action begins with the player to the left of the big blind. (Players can either call, raise, or fold.)

continue...

After the first round of betting, 3 cards are dealt on the table (called the "flop"). These "community cards" are for all players to use at the same time to try to create the best poker hand. After another betting round, 1 more community card is dealt ("turn"), followed by another betting round and then the last community card "river". After another round of betting, all remaining players in their hand must reveal their cards. The player with the best 5-card poker hand (consisting of any combination of their 2 hole cards and 5 community cards) wins.
